Artist's Statement

My name is Alexandra Baker and I am a digital media artist. I care about connecting with people and being able to share relatable topics such as media art. In this type of art I am looking more in to taking footage and turning them in to GIF in which I can use for web based topics, like having an image like the one on here (a person pointing and gesturing) pointing to links to be clicked to a linked page. How I will do this is another question I will be focusing on because there are many way to do it. However, the endgame will be to produce a web page with interactivity in which clikable links will open other pages to different subjects.
