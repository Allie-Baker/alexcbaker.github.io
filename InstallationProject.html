Artist's Statement

Hi there, I am Alexandra Baker and I am a Digital Media artist. I care about how I create and edit my work so that I can have a art piece for an audience to interpret and maybe even relate to. I am focusing my work on travelling to places were I can take footage of landscapes and people. I am aiming to have relatable content for people to view and to document my life and my journey. The way I look at the world is mostly through a lens, capturing and documenting what I see which are places I go to because I am a traveler and an individual who loves culture and nature. I want to be able to share my world, through a lens (camera or video), and show what I see and give the opportunity for others to relate to it.It would be a my goal to see mountains and oceans from other continents and get amazing footage to show the world.
